About us

Welcome to HAPPY PAWS Pet Adoption, where every pet is treated like family. Founded in 2024 by a group of animal enthusiasts and welfare advocates, our organization has become a beacon of hope for pets awaiting their forever homes. We are a non-profit, community-driven group fueled by the passion for making a difference in the lives of animals and the people who love them.


Our facility is a home-away-from-home for pets in transition, and our dedicated team works tirelessly to provide the care, love, and attention that our furry friends need. From the moment they arrive, each pet is given a comprehensive health check, rehabilitated both physically and emotionally, and cared for by our staff and volunteers until the perfect match is found.


Beyond adoptions, we offer a range of services to our community, including educational programs on responsible pet ownership, spaying/neutering campaigns to help control the pet population, and a pet food pantry to assist pet owners facing economic hardships.


At HAPPY PAWS Pet Adoption, we believe that by working together, we can create a world where every pet is safe, respected, and loved. We invite you to join us on this journey, whether it's by adopting, volunteering, donating, or simply spreading the word. Together, we can make a lasting impact on the lives of countless pets and people.
